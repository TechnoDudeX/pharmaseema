About Seema

A Pharmacist Who Truly Cares

I'm a licensed pharmacist passionate about patients and the future of pharmacy. Over the years, I've worked across community and compounding settings — helping people understand their medications and feel genuinely supported in their care.

Today, I specialize in senior care as a Clinical Consultant Pharmacist at CareRx Corporation, where I focus on medication safety for retirement homes and long-term care facilities. Ensuring that seniors receive the safest, most effective care is my highest priority.
